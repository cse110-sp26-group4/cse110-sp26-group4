# AI Slot Machine

A fun, AI-themed slot machine game built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## How to Play

1.  Open the `index.html` file in your web browser.
2.  You start with 1000 tokens.
3.  Set your bet amount in the "Bet" field.
4.  Click the "Spin" button to play.
5.  If you get a winning combination, you'll win tokens based on the payout table.
6.  After a win, you can click the "Gamble" button to try and double your winnings. Be careful, you could also lose them!

## Payouts

- 🤖🤖🤖: 50x your bet
- 🧠🧠🧠: 20x your bet
- 🚀🚀🚀: 15x your bet
- 💻💻💻: 10x your bet
- 🪙🪙🪙: 5x your bet
- Any two matching symbols + 🤖: 3x your bet
- Any two matching symbols + 🧠: 2x your bet

Have fun!
